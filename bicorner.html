BB Corner

Bangladesh Bank Corner is one of the facilities available in the Noakhali Science and Technology University library. Bangladesh Bank Corner has been there since the NSTU library was established.

Students of Noakhali Science and Technology University often use the Bangladesh Bank Corner as a place for discussions and group work. This is because the Bangladesh Bank Corner in the NSTU library provides facilities such as sofas, televisions, and computers. It also features a red carpet, giving an elegant impression to passersby. Additionally, the Bangladesh Bank Corner offers a wide collection of books that students can read.
