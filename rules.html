Rules of NSTU University Library

    Here are the rules that must be followed in the NSTU University library:

  1. It is forbidden to bring food or drinks into the library.
  2. Bags are not allowed inside the library; if necessary, they can be stored in the locker.
  3. If you want to store your bag or belongings in the locker, exchange your student ID card for a locker key at the front office.
  4. To borrow a book, first register as a borrower at the front office.
  5. The maximum borrowing period for books is one week (a fine will be imposed if overdue).
  6. Discuss in a low voice, not loudly.
